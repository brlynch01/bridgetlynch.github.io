Geoscience as a vector for equity

I am interested in studying the intersection and interactions between communities and their environments. Whether it be through risk communication or outreach with youth interested in outdoor work, I believe in utilizing tools that the geosciences offer in order to help create a safer, more sustainabily habitable and equitable world for all people.

Education

I am anticipated to graduate from Wellesley College with a major in Geosciences and minor in Environmental Studies in May 2024. 

Experience

I have experience working in a variety of lab settings at Wellesley College and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, as well as several field-bound positions including survey work for a federal land management agency and world-renowned botanic garden and arboretum.
